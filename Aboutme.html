2015

Born in raised in New Jersey, I fell in love with the arts. My art class in highschool was the only reason I really enjoyed school. By my jr year I qualified to become an independent study in Ceramics, my work went on to be displayed in the public library.

2016

I moved to New York to live with my grandparents in hopes that in the city their would be more opportunities. I joined many internships, from street art to photography. There was not a medium of the arts I didnt excel in.

2017

I started to dive into the world of social media, first as a model than as a photographer. The algorithm became something I wanted to learn and master. As I started to learn social media , I saw flaws and wanted to develop my own application.

2018

I put together a small collective called Unsigned Creators. Within that collective I would post flyers hosting castings for youth who wanted to put together short films and get their photo taken. This collective was started to inspire everyday creatives to feel like they can be seen on social media and their creativity is vaild.

2019

I went on to look for a mentor who would be willing to teach me how to code.Once I found my mentor , whom was a college professor at rutgers, it was time to begin a new journey. For years I never looked at myself as anything other than an artist. Learning computer science was a challenge withmany rewards.

2020

After one semester in computer science , I didnt think that major was menat for me. It was very exciting to find out that BMCC offers a Multimedia Programming and Design Major. After many years of trying out alot of different things. I stuumbled upon my true passion. Which is interior design, especially eco friendly design. Outside of school I study interior design in hopes to transfer after graduating with my associates.
